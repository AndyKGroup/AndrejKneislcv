                How It Works

                • Personal information updates all sections of the app immediately. Fields left empty are hidden automatically — no broken placeholders.
                • ATS Mode keeps your CV export clean, structured, and text-first. No photos or decorative elements appear in ATS exports.
                • Premium Mode enables your profile photo in the Pitch Deck and on the CV header (when a valid photo path is set and Show Photo is on).
                • The Shareable Public Link opens the app in recruiter-facing view with your selected language pre-applied. The Settings tab is hidden.
                • PDF exports use your current language and reflect all active settings. The CV export is rendered as a clean print document.
                • Settings and personal information are saved locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.
